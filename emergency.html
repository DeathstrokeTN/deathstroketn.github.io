🚨 Emergency Guidance

Know exactly when to stop managing at home and seek immediate medical or psychiatric help.

1 Psychiatric Emergency

Seek help immediately if the patient:

  • ❗ Threatens self-harm or suicide.
  • ❗ Threatens to harm you or others.
  • ❗ Severe hallucinations (hearing/seeing things).
  • ❗ Stops eating/drinking for multiple days.
  • ❗ Unresponsive but breathing.

2 Medical Emergency

Call an ambulance if you notice:

  • ⚡ Face drooping on one side.
  • ⚡ Arm weakness (cannot raise both).
  • ⚡ Speech difficulty or slurring.
  • ⚡ Sudden, severe headache.
  • ⚡ Sudden loss of balance or severe fall.
Note: Time is critical. Remember F.A.S.T. (Face, Arms, Speech, Time).
