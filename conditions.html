Understanding Conditions

Learn about common conditions, what symptoms to expect, and mistakes to avoid.

⚕️ Stroke Recovery

📋 Symptoms to Expect

  • 🔹 Physical weakness on one side
  • 🔹 Difficulty speaking (Aphasia)
  • 🔹 Emotional changes & frustration
  • 🔹 Significant fatigue

💡 Inside the Mind

The brain is healing. Recovery takes time. Their outbursts are often a result of brain injury, not their true feelings.

⚠️ Mistakes to Avoid

Avoid

Doing everything for them. Let them rebuild independence.

Avoid

Speaking louder if they have aphasia. They can hear you; speak slowly.

🌧️ Depression

📋 Symptoms to Expect

  • 🔹 Loss of interest in hobbies
  • 🔹 Persistent sadness or irritability
  • 🔹 Appetite and sleep changes
  • 🔹 Chronic lack of energy

💡 Inside the Mind

Depression is an illness, not a choice. They can't "snap out of it." Small tasks feel like mountains.

⚠️ Mistakes to Avoid

Avoid

Saying "cheer up." It only increases their guilt.

Avoid

Taking their social isolation personally.
